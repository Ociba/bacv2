    • Bukalasa Agricultural College Background

    Bukalasa Agricultural College is situated 50 km north of Kampala; it was established by the then Colonial Government in 1929 as a cotton breeding centre. It has since then metamorphosed into an agricultural training institution with a strong reputation of training front line agricultural extension workers as well as a home to many household names of academicians, administrators, researchers, and impeccable leaders.

    Recently international students are getting attracted to the college to pursue various academic programmes, thus widening the scope of the institution in coverage. The college wishes to ensure that the original mandate is not lost, and at the same time rise to the challenges of satisfying needs of the expanding clientele. This situation is covered in the Vision, Mission, Core Values and Objectives below.

    BAC
    VISION : A leading institution in skilling Agricultural Professionals, Practitioners and Agro-Entrepreneurs.
    Mission : To train and skill using innovative approaches for Sustainable Agricultural Transformation.
    Goal : To produce competent agricultural and agro-industrial practioners and farming community
    Goal : that is engaged in promotion of farming as a profitable business and honorable profession.
    Motto : Agriculture for Health and Wealth.

    The Core Values

    1. Diversity and Inclusion - We honor the individual. We celebrate differences and use them to create unity

    2. Integrity - We believe that trust is a fundamental requisite of any undertaking. It’s about being open, honest and respectful at all times with our colleagues, with our customers and with our stakeholders, and the public.

    3. Accountability – We are obligated to account for our activities, meet our commitments and accept our individual and team responsibilities. We take responsibility for our performance in all of our decisions and actions.

    4. Innovation – We anticipate change and shape it to fit our purposes. We identify, develop and deploy leading edge technology, employee development programs and process improvement tools.

    5. Team Work – We believe in working and growing together for the common good. We are one team, committed to a teamwork environment where every person is a valued member, treated with respect, encouraged to contribute and recognized and rewarded for his/her efforts.

    The Objectives

    1. To initiate and promote innovations in Agricultural Training Programs.

    2. To produce skilled manpower with positive attitude to serve the Agricultural Sector and Society.

    3. To equip trainees, with scientific knowledge and skills that they can independently apply in the field.

    4. To equip trainees with analytical tools required to solve problems along the agricultural value chains.

    5. To promote the acquisition, adaptation and application of information technology to address the social and economic challenges of Uganda.

    6. To support establishment of a resource center for the production and dissemination of information relating to Agriculture and related disciplines.

    7. To undertake research for the generation of knowledge required to advance the Agricultural Sector

    8. To support community development through the outreach programme.
